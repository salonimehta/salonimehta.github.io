Colours of the Campus

by Anjana, Herold, Nupur and Saloni for Information Design assignment - 'Mental Cities'

Spread over 550 acres, the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay (Mumbai, IN) is a riot of colours. On one hand the land is
surrounded by scenic hills, lakes and green cover, and on another it supports the institute's various activities like academics,
sports, cultural, housing, et. al. We have tried to capture this plethora of colour by dividing the institute into unit blocks and
visually determing the colour felt most strongly in the area.  (You can hover over the map below to see through the colours)
