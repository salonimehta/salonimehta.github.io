Hi, I am Saloni.

As an empathetic designer, I like to create experiences that become natural extensions of oneself. My left brain has hot spots that make me detail oriented while my right brain enables me to be intuitive and a good listener. I strategise my workflow to adhere to the problem in hand and value collaboration - with other designers, developers and the users themselves.

When I’m not in front of my laptop creating solutions, you can find me sipping a cup of masala chai with my nose in a book, someplace, somewhere (with my ever faithful camera).
